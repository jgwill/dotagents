# AGENTS.md - Deep Research Foundations

## Purpose

This skill is the Storyweaver / Miadi refactoring-portal fork of `deep-research`.
It turns external inquiry into durable `foundations/<academic-field>/` artifacts
that can serve three audiences at once:

- academic readers: fields, concepts, citations, cautions
- engineering readers: architecture, source ledgers, data-model implications
- narrative readers: chronicle seeds, voice lines, visual motifs

## Inquiry relation

Ask: what recognized academic/professional fields does this design decomposition imply,
and what should we search externally so the branch can teach itself without drifting into
unsupported lore?
